About Passquick

If you are having lessons on a weekly basis but you just want to get your license swiftly then you should think about an intensive driving course. We offer intensive courses over shorter time period or can modify your intensive driving course to suit your current driving ability.

If you have passed the theory test and you need to pass fast for whatever reason - work - pregnancy - time restrictions, then we offer an intensive driving course, We pride ourselves in giving quality lessons centred around the pupils, with 20 year’s of teaching experience, the lessons are taught in a relaxed atmosphere and are structured around the pupil, we cater for all pupils i.e with learning difficulties, anxiety etc

All Passquik driving instructors are fully qualified ADIs, they all have up to date CRB’s. We only use modern cars for our intensive driving courses , with all the usual safety features of a learner car; such as dual controls. You can be rest assured your fully qualified Driving Standards Agency approved driving instructor will make sure you are not only capable of passing your test but have all the skills for safe driving for life.
